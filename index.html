The Wolfhound Bar and Kitchen

Craft Cocktails. Scratch Kitchen. Good Times.

Since opening in Hovember 2021, The Wolfhound Bar and Kitchen has provided an exceptional dining experience in an intimate but inviting space. With delicious craft made cocktails, rotating multiple local beers on draft, and quality pours of imported favorites from our bar- we take pride in our drinks and work hard to bring a unique and thoughtful experience. Our kitcken delivers authentic, traditional plates with care. Plan an experience with The Wolfhound at our home in Chicago's Avondale neighborhood!
